Target Audience:

This site will be frequented by locals and visitors to the area who are interested in weather conditions, particularly as they affect outdoor activities, and in local events.

Persona One:

Group Outdoorsman
Fictional name Brian Mills
Activities Hiking, fishing, and camping at and around Bear Lake for days at a time
Demographics Age 55, male, married
Tasks for site Checking for storms, predicted high and low temperatures, and wind information for his trips.
Environment Accesses the site from his desktop computer. Can get around on the internet but not comfortable with technology.
Quote "It's great to get away from it all."

Persona Two:

Group Young adult
Fictional name Jessica Palmer
Activities Day trips with friends, picnics, day hikes, and paticipating in regional faires and festivities.
Demographics Age 21, female, single
Tasks for site Checking for upcoming events, for predicted temperatures and for expected rain that may affect her outings with friends.
Environment Uses her laptop and smartphone regularly to access the site. Always has her smartphone with her. Very comfortable with technology.
Quote "Come on! Let's go do something fun today!"

Possible scenarios:

  • What is the expected wind chill factor for Fish Haven on Saturday?
  • Is there a storm approaching this weekend? How hard will the wind be blowing?
  • What are the predicted overnight lows for the next three days?
  • Is it supposed to rain during my day hike tomorrow? How heavily?
  • What upcoming local festivals can I attend? What are the dates? Will there be food for sale?
