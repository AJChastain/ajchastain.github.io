Target audience:

This site will be frequented by locals and visitors to the area who are interested in weather conditions, particularly as they affect outdoor activities, and in local events.

A 35-year-old outdoorsman who often hikes, camps, and fishes at and around Bear Lake, along which Fish Haven is located. He accesses the website from his home desktop computer prior to embarking on his trips.

Persona Two:

A 21-year-old woman who likes to participate in local events and frequently takes local day trips with her friends. She accesses the website from her smartphone while making plans for something to do with her friends.
