Typography

Font 1: Merienda One

The font I used for the header, nav bar, headings, and footer is the cursive font "Merienda One". Merienda One is a suitable font because it looks slightly windblown and is yet perfectly readable. This ties in with the Wind Check theme and differentiates between headings and body content.


Font 2: PT Serif

The body content is in the serif font "PT Serif". Serifs are ideal for body text because they are easy on the readers' eyes and don't cause eye strain as swiftly as other font styles do. When reading large amounts of text, a serif is the best choice. PT Serif is a clean, classic serif with full, round letters that make it easy for readers to stay on the page.
