Site Motto:

Site Purpose:

Wind Check is a great site for staying up-to-date on local weather and events in the southeastern Idaho area. Adventurers, explorers, and locals alike can depend on Wind Check for current weather conditions in Preston, Fish Haven, and Soda Springs. Is this a perfect day for a hike? Is there a storm expected that might affect the camping trip you planned for this weekend? Are there any festivals or faires coming up soon? Visit Wind Check to find out, and you can be ready for anything!
