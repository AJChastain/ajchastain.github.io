Spring Fling Community Event

Saturday, May 8th, from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.

at Kelly Park in Soda Springs

As we bid winter adieu and head eagerly into a new season, we have the welcome opportunity to crawl out of our icy retreats and celebrate together. This year, the Spring Fling will be better than ever before.

Held in the beautiful Kelly Park, the Spring Fling will be the host of many games and activities for revelers of all ages. There will be classic faire food available for purchase, free wagon rides, and a ring toss competition. Kite Flying in the Field is always a family favorite! Bring your kite or purchase a one there. The Pioneer Princesses will offer basket-weaving lessons for the cost of materials. (Yes, we‚Äôre serious. It‚Äôs so fun!) There will also be a presentation from the local Geological Society about the unique rocks and minerals to be found in our area.

As always, Kelly Park also has hiking trails to explore, a bike park, and fishing ponds. Come early, stay late, and enjoy the beauty of springtime with other happy people from our community and beyond. Bring your family and friends!
