Membership Levels

Non-profit ($100/year) includes:

  • ✓ Directory listing on our website in the Local Business Guide
  • ✓ Monthly posts for your organization on our social media sites
  • ✓ Your ad in the monthly mail flyer

Bronze ($200/year) includes:

  • ✓ Directory listing on our website in the Local Business Guide
  • ✓ Monthly posts for your business on our social media sites
  • ✓ Your ad in the monthly mail flyer
  • ✓ Monthly business seminars

Silver ($400/year) includes:

  • ✓ Directory listing on our website in the Local Business Guide
  • ✓ Weekly posts for your business on our social media sites
  • ✓ Your ad in the monthly mail flyer
  • ✓ Semi-annual Area Business Leaders' Lunch
  • ✓ Monthly business seminars
  • ✓ Specialized web training

Gold ($1000/year) includes:

  • ✓ Directory listing on our website and in the Local Business Guide
  • ✓ Daily posts for your business on our social media sites
  • ✓ Your ad in the monthly mail flyer
  • ✓ An advertisement on our Home Page
  • ✓ Monthly Area Business Leaders Lunch
  • ✓ Special discounts on local services
